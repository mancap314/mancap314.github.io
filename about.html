A bit about this blog and me...

Hello, welcome to my blog, thanks for visiting! I am Manuel Capel, working as a data scientist in the Zurich financial center.

This blog keeps track of experiments, readings etc. I found interesting, especially in topics regarding machine/deep learning. Among them, Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Reinforcement Learning (RL) are my favorits I must confess, but others are also interesting. I have been working myself around data since several years by now, as research assistent, data integration specialist and data scientist. If I can help you, just tell me.

Since a while I am also interested in computer viruses. It's a quite fascinating topics. Not to destroy anything, don't worry, besides hopefully the fantasies around them. There is no reason that only evil guys and some specialists study them.

Otherwise in my free time I like to play ping-pong and chess, both are (almost?) addictions, meet friends, from time to time read and travel...
